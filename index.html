Paragraphs & Topic Sentences. A paragraph is a series of sentences that are organized and coherent, and are all related to a single topic.

Heading

There's no defined length - number of lines, words or sentences - for 'paragraph'.
Paragraphs are often five to eight lines in length,
but frequently they're shorter or longer.
What's more important than word count is the quality and flow of the paragraph.
Paragraphs are often thought of as a 'unit' of thought.
